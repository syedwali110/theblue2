Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is The Blue Karachi located? →
The Blue Karachi is located in Clifton, Karachi — one of the city's most prestigious and accessible neighbourhoods, close to major roads and public transport routes.
What are the parking facilities? →
We have over 3,000 parking spaces across 2 basement levels and a dedicated multi-storey car park. Valet parking is available at all entrances for Gold members and above.
Is there a prayer area available? →
Yes, dedicated prayer halls with ablution facilities are located on Level 2 and Level 5. They are open throughout mall hours and are climate controlled.
How do I join the Rewards Club? →
Sign up for free at the Concierge Desk on the Ground Floor, at any participating store, or online at thebluekarachi.com. You start earning points from your very first purchase.
Is the mall accessible for people with disabilities? →
Absolutely. The Blue is fully accessible with dedicated parking, wide corridors, accessible restrooms on every floor, lifts, and trained staff to assist visitors with special needs.
Can I host a private event at The Blue? →
Yes! We have several event spaces from intimate boardrooms to the spectacular Grand Atrium accommodating up to 2,000 guests. Contact our events team at events@thebluekarachi.com.
